ABOUT

VOLRACE was born in 2017 to respond to the growing demand for spaces where motor enthusiasts could exhibit, talk, participate and enjoy professional events, with engine noise as the protagonist. The inspiration for this type of event has been to bring together the best most extreme National and International Drift along with super exclusive vehicles to enter the track and a great exhibition opening the range to all types of motor vehicles. VOLRACE is passion for motors in all its disciplines. Exhibition, sport, meeting and promotion as entertainment in the Classic, Racing, Drift, Supercars, Grip and Drag Race Acceleration categories.
