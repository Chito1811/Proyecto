ABOUT

VOLRACE was born in 2017 to respond to the growing demand for spaces where motor enthusiasts could exhibit, talk, participate and enjoy professional events, with engine noise as the protagonist.

The inspiration for this type of event has been to bring together the best most extreme National and International Drift along with super exclusive vehicles to enter the track and a great exhibition opening the range to all types of motor vehicles. ...

VOLRACE is passion for motors in all its disciplines. Exhibition, sport, meeting and promotion as entertainment in the Classic, Racing, Drift, Supercars, Grip and Drag Race Acceleration categories.

DRIFTING

Complete routes to Drift with the most exclusive cars from all over Europe and prepared in the world with top-level pilots such as Alex Claudin, participant of the series Netflix of car tests.

Our Drift tests are very radical and exclusive. Not everyone can enter them, creating a great level and a spectacle like no other, seeing cars drifting at ... more than 250 km/h and with powers of more than 1,500 hp.

In these World Cup circuits only specialists manage to do this type of test successfully and thanks to our professionalism and experience we are able to create unique events in each of them.
